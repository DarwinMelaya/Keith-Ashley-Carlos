My Favorite Foods

Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish made from thinly sliced, marinated meat (usually chicken, beef, or lamb) that is slow-cooked on a vertical rotisserie. It's typically served in flatbread, like pita, and garnished with vegetables, sauces, and condiments.

Fries

Fries are thinly sliced potatoes that are deep-fried until crispy and golden, often served as a snack or side dish, commonly seasoned with salt.

Chocolates

Chocolates are sweet treats made from cocoa beans, often mixed with sugar and milk. They come in various forms, such as bars, truffles, and candies, and are enjoyed for their rich, smooth flavor.
