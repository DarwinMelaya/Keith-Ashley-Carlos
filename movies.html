Un/Happy for You

“Un/Happy for You” is a 2024 Filipino romantic drama film directed by Petersen Vargas. The story follows Juancho, a rising Bicolano chef, who reunites with his ex-girlfriend Zy during her brief work visit to their hometown. Initially seeking revenge for their past breakup, Juancho finds himself falling for Zy once more, complicating her engagement

Clueless | "whatever"

“Clueless” is a 1995 American teen comedy film directed by Amy Heckerling. It stars Alicia Silverstone as Cher Horowitz, a wealthy and popular high school student in Beverly Hills. Cher navigates the ups and downs of teenage life, including matchmaking for her teachers, giving a makeover to a new student, and discovering her own feelings and identity. The film is known for its witty dialogue, iconic fashion, and memorable catchphrases like "Whatever!"

She’s the Man: Womanizer

In the movie “She’s the Man”, the “Womanizer” scene features Viola, disguised as her twin brother Sebastian, trying to earn respect from her soccer teammates. With the help of her friend Paul, she stages a scenario where several girls pretend to be her ex-girlfriends, each praising “Sebastian’s” charm. This clever ruse helps Viola gain credibility and admiration among her peers
