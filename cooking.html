The Food I Always Cook:

Carbonara

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with a creamy sauce of eggs, cheese (typically Pecorino Romano), cured pork (like guanciale or pancetta), and black pepper. It's known for its rich flavor and silky texture, often served with spaghetti or fettuccine.

Sizzling Tofu

Sizzling Tofu is a popular Asian dish featuring tofu cubes that are marinated and then stir-fried until crispy. It's typically served on a hot plate, creating a delightful sizzling sound. The dish often includes a mix of colorful vegetables and a savory sauce, offering a flavorful and satisfying vegetarian option.

Sipo Eggs

Sipo Eggs are a popular traditional snack from Nigeria, often made by boiling eggs and then frying them in a spicy tomato sauce. The dish is known for its rich flavor, combining spices and seasonings that enhance the taste of the eggs. It is often enjoyed as a street food or appetizer.
